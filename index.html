Hello World!

My name is Skyler Seto and I'm a third year P.H.D. student in the Department of Statistical Sciences at Cornell University. I graduated from MIT in 2014 with a degree in Mathematics with Computer Science, and received an I.B. diploma from King High School in 2010.

My research interests include statistical machine learning, probablistic modeling, approximate inference algorithms, and big data. My current research focuses probablistic methods for inferring local ancestry.
